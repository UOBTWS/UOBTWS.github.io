Introduciton

The University of Birmingham Taiwanese Society upholds the charge of enhancing the general welfare of our members with the operation and maintenance of various communication channels, the holding of social events and activities, and the provision of services which may be necessary and ameliorative to our members whether they be new-comers to the City of Birmingham or students in times of need.

We hope, to date, that we have done so with efficacy and integrity; and shall endeavor to do so with continuity.

Any concerns or questions may be referred to our executive team aforesaid; or to our members on the board who have always, with diligence,shown their willingness to help; the soul of our community away from home.
